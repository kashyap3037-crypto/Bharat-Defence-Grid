Historic Operations

1947–1948

Air Operations (Kashmir)

Vital airlift and combat support during the first Indo-Pak war, ensuring early tactical superiority.

1962

Air Support (Indo-China)

Critical logistics, transport, and supply missions across extremely challenging high-altitude terrain.

1965

Indo-Pak War

Establishment of air superiority over West Pakistan and heroic support during the Battle of Asal Uttar.

Dec 1971

1971 War Campaigns

Decisive strikes including the legendary Battle of Longewala, where the IAF decimated an armored regiment.

1999

Operation Safed Sagar

The first high-altitude operation of its kind, using precision strikes to evict infiltrators from Kargil heights.

26 Feb 2019

Balakot Airstrike

A precision cross-border pre-emptive strike on major terror launchpads, demonstrating deep-strike capability.

2022

Operation Ganga

Massive strategic airlift to rescue thousands of Indian students from the conflict zone in Ukraine.

2025

Operation Sindoor

A joint Indian Army–Air Force precision strike targeting terrorist infrastructure with high accuracy.
