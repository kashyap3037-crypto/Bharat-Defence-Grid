INDIAN ARMY

Service Before Self — The primary land-based branch of the Indian Armed Forces.

Back to Dashboard

Key Assets

  • Main Battle Tanks: T-90 Bhishma, Arjun MK1A, T-72 Ajeya.
  • Artillery: Dhanush 155mm, K9-Vajra, Pinaka MBRL.
  • Aviation: HAL LCH Prachand, Rudra, AH-64E Apache.
  • Infantry: SIG716 Rifles, AK-203, Advanced Ballistic Gear.

Mission

To safeguard India’s sovereignty and territorial integrity by defending land borders against external threats and maintaining internal security.

Historic Operations

1947–1948

Indo-Pak War

Defence of Jammu & Kashmir against invasion; First major conflict post-independence.

1948

Operation Polo

Successful military operation to integrate Hyderabad into the Indian Union.

1962

Indo-China War

Border conflict in Ladakh and NEFA; Heroic stands at Rezang La.

1965

Indo-Pak War

Large-scale conflict over Kashmir; Successful defense against Operation Gibraltar.

1971

1971 War (Liberation of Bangladesh)

Decisive victory leading to the birth of Bangladesh and the largest military surrender since WWII.

1984 - Present

Operation Meghdoot

Strategic capture and continued defense of the Siachen Glacier, the world's highest battlefield.

1999

Operation Vijay (Kargil War)

High-altitude war to evict Pakistani regulars from fortified positions in Kargil.

2016

Surgical Strikes

Precision cross-border strikes on terror launchpads in PoK following the Uri attack.

Strategic Focus

  • High-Altitude Warfare: Dominance in regions like Siachen and Ladakh.
  • Internal Security: Counter-terrorism & counter-insurgency operations.
  • Integrated Battle Groups: Rapid mobilization & agile combat deployments.
  • Tech Integration: Modernization with AI, drones, and advanced surveillance.
  • Civil Support: Providing rapid relief during disasters and emergencies.
