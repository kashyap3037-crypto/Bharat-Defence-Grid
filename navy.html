INDIAN NAVY

Sham No Varunah — May the Lord of Water be auspicious unto us.

Back to Dashboard

Key Assets

  • Carriers: INS Vikramaditya, INS Vikrant.
  • Submarines: Kalvari-class, Arihant-class (Nuclear).
  • Destroyers: Visakhapatnam-class, Kolkata-class.
  • Aviation: MiG-29K, P-8I Poseidon, MH-60R Seahawk.

Mission

To secure India’s maritime borders, protect sea lanes, and ensure dominance in the Indian Ocean Region.

Historic Operations

4 Dec 1971

Operation Trident

A daring missile boat attack on Karachi harbour, inflicting heavy damage on the Pakistani Navy.

8 Dec 1971

Operation Python

The successful follow-up naval strike on Karachi, solidifying Indian naval dominance in the Arabian Sea.

1999

Operation Talwar

The naval blockade of Pakistani ports during the Kargil War, effectively cutting off their supply lines.

2006

Operation Sukoon

The largest maritime evacuation mission since WWII, rescuing Indian, Sri Lankan, and Nepalese nationals from Lebanon.

2008 – Present

Anti-Piracy (Gulf of Aden)

Continuous patrol and protection of global shipping lanes against maritime piracy.

2015

Operation Rahat

Heroic evacuation of over 4,600 Indian citizens and 960 foreign nationals from conflict-torn Yemen.

2020 – Present

Mission SAGAR

Strategic deployment to provide food, COVID-related medicines, and medical support to IOR island nations.

Strategic Focus

  • Blue-Water Expansion: Achieving global reach and persistent presence.
  • Carrier Battle Groups: Operationalizing carriers and advanced submarine fleets.
  • Domain Awareness: Anti-piracy operations and securing sea lanes.
  • Strategic Deterrence: strengthening underwater nuclear capabilities.
  • HADR: Humanitarian assistance & disaster relief globally.
